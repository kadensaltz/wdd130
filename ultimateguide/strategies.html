Strategies

H-Stack

H-stack or horizontal stack is when you have three handlers who are best at throwing, and 4 cutters who have high endurance, speed, and are good at catching the frisbee. The handlers and cutter line up horizontally and then the cutters move like pistons cutting up and down the field. Once a succesful pass is made forward the handlers run up and the frisbee is thrown back to them and the pattern resets farther up the field.

V-Stack

V-Stack or vertical stack is when there are two handlers and 5 cutters. The cutters line up vertically and make cuts either towards the handlers, left or right of the stack, or deep to catch a long pass. Same as with H-stack once a pass is complete the cutter returns the disc to a handler and the stack resets
