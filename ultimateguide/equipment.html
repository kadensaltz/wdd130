Equipment to get started

Frisbees

The first think you will need of course is a frisbee. In ultiamte there is a size and weight required to be an official playing disc. It must weigh 175g and have a diamter of 27.5cm.

Standard Ultimate discs

Cleats

Not only is it safer to play with cleats its also much easier. If you want to be able to play effectively you will need a good pair of cleats.

Cleats

Friction Gloves

These gloves make catching the frisbee and throwing it much easier. They are a must have if you are playing ultimate for a few hours as they also help protect your fingers and hands.
